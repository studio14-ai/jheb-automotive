About JHEB

The independent workshop locals trust.

JHEB Automotive is built for drivers who want proper mechanical work, no matter what they drive. From logbook servicing and everyday maintenance to major repairs and full engine rebuilds, we bring the experience, tools, and technical know-how to get it done properly.

We also specialise in performance and aftermarket work. Our team has undertaken additional training to carry out upgrades and installs that go beyond standard servicing, including boost controllers, gauges, interior lighting, head units, subwoofers, turbochargers, and complete engine swaps.

We’re comfortable taking on the jobs other workshops avoid. If it’s complex, unusual, or hasn’t been done before, that’s often where we do our best work.
